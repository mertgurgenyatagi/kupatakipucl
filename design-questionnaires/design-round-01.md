# Kupatakip UCL — Design Questionnaire, Round 1

Vague gut-check round, filled out via the design-intake tool.

---

**1. Tool vs. group chat.** Quick gut check: is this closer to a serious stats tool, or a group chat that happens to have a leaderboard bolted on?

Your answer: Stats tool, all the way

---

**2. Physical place.** If kupatakip UCL were a physical place — a room, a stadium section, a specific bar — where would it be?

Your answer: A luxurious, classy office in NYC where the top executives are hanging out after hours, drinking whiskey.

---

**3. Dark vs. light.** Dark, light, or depends on the section?

Your answer: Light

---

**4. Borrowed feel.** Name one app, game, or website — doesn't have to be sports-related — whose FEEL you'd quietly want to steal. What's the one thing about it?

Your answer: Honestly? "linear.app". Just does most things right. Great toolbar at the top, not extremely comprehensive animations but not totally static either. Stuff I don't like about it is it's dark mode and also it's not giving the optimistic "google to do app" energy of dopamine hits. Also love The Telegraph.

---

**5. Leaderboard moment.** Someone jumps to #1 on the leaderboard. Should the site make a big deal of it, or stay cool about it?

Your answer: Stay cool — just move the name up

---

**6. Color gut-check.** Any color already in your head? “Something red”, “no idea”, and an exact hex are all fine answers.

Your answer: UEFA colors. Non-negotiable.

---

**7. Type feel.** Type feel: chunky and bold, sharp and thin, or rounded and friendly?

Your answer: Sharp & thin

---

**8. Old kupatakip.** Anything about the OLD kupatakip's look you'd actually keep, if anything?

Your answer: Very intuitive. We can't compromise on intuitive.

---

**9. Broadcast vs. Discord.** Closer to ESPN / a real broadcast graphics package, or closer to a private Discord server's own skin?

Your answer: Not sure

---

**10. Worst-case look.** Last one: anything you're scared it'll end up looking like? The thing you'd hate seeing.

Your answer: AI slop. Award winning websites that advertise products and are trying to get out of your monitor. Pessimistic shit. Anything that is not intuitive.
