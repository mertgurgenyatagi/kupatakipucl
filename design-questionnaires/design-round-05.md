# Kupatakip UCL — Design Questionnaire, Round 5

More vibes, filled out via the design-intake tool.

**1. Player photos.** Real player photos are in — full color, black & white, or slightly muted/desaturated?

Your answer: Slightly muted

---

**2. The confetti sound.** The one deliberate celebration on the whole site — the eventual season winner's confetti — if it had a sound, what is it?

Your answer: A low-end glass choir of whoosh.

---

**3. A quiet stretch.** Nobody's opened the site in a few quiet days. Does that empty stretch feel like a problem, or just normal?

Your answer: Just normal, no big deal

---

**4. A fourth object.** Executive pen, a plaque, fresh car smell — one more object joins that collection. What is it?

Your answer: Weird answer, it might wake you from your preconceptions: a crowd of passionate football fans.

---

**5. Chat/forum energy.** The chat/forum pages — exactly the same buttoned-up personality as the leaderboard, or does it loosen up since it's just friends talking?

Your answer: Loosens up a microscopic bit.

---

**6. Room for a joke.** Should the site ever crack a joke anywhere, or stay completely straight-faced throughout?

Your answer: Rarely, one or two spots

---

**7. The phone buzz.** Someone jumps ahead of you on the leaderboard and your phone buzzes. In your own words, what does that notification say?

Your answer: Well, first of all, no notifications. But it would say: "Kuzey has taken your spot."

---

**8. Dressed for the occasion.** If the whole site were dressed for the occasion: a suit, a blazer with no tie, a full tux, or something else?

Your answer: White shirt, buttoned down slightly.

**9. A mark of its own.** Should the site have a little mark/icon of its own — a shield, a ball, initials, something abstract — or no real preference?

Your answer: You don't want to know the answer. Sshh. Just skip it.

**10. Anything else.** Anything else sitting in your head, unformed or not?
